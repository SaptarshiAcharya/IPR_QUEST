About The Game


Copycat Clan, an evil organization is spreading their virus "Xeron" that makes people mimic each other profusely.This virus is spreading rapidly and has taken all over the country. Dr. Raman has come to the rescue with a groundbreaking intervention, a drug that promotes individuality in artistic manifestations.


Embark on a fascinating adventure with Dr. Raman, the creator of "Anti-Xeron" to save the world from the spell of The Copycat Clan. Help him leverage Intellectual Property Rights and attain his goal of encouraging uniqueness in creative expression.
